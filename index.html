Our story

Built on a manufacturing floor, not a sales deck.

Taglyne Co. is the new export-facing identity of a family business that has produced woven labels and printed tapes for local Pakistani brands for years. We're now opening that production capability to buyers abroad — backed by real manufacturing, not a middleman markup.

Alongside our own production, we work with a trusted third-party printing partner for hang tags, care labels and stickers — giving us the flexibility to turn samples around in hours, not weeks.
